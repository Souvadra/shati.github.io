About me

Hi! I'm a Computational Biologist at the Van Allen Lab at Dana-Farber Cancer Institute. Prior to joining the lab, I graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi in 2020 with a B. Tech in Biochemical Engineering and Biotechnology.

I am interested in studying resistance mechanisms in oncology, and the inter and intra-tumor patient heterogeneity in clinical samples through the lens of single cell sequencing. I am also interested in developing new methods and pipelines for single-cell analysis.
